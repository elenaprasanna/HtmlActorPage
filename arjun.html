Arjun Das

Indian Tamil Actor

Arjun Das is an Indian actor who predominantly works in Tamil films, along with a few Telugu films. Best known for his role as Anbu in the film Kaithi (2019), he has garnered major attention for his bass voice. Arjun made his acting debut in the suspense thriller Perumaan (2012). Following a few other movies and short films, he appeared in Lokesh Kanagaraj's Kaithi, portraying the film's antagonist, a cocaine addict gang leader and then collaborated with Lokesh again for the film Master portraying Das, the film's second hero.
